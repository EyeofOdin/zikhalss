About Us

Established 2021 | Registration: 2021/666486/07

Professional Security Solutions

Zikhal Security Service is a BBBEE Level 1 company which has served a wide range of clients from virtually every industry, including multinational businesses, and clients from the mining and hospitality industries.

ZSS Security Officers are meticulously screened and well trained. They provide services to various private companies and public entities. These hand-picked officers are trained in basic security functions, professional attitude and appearance, public relations and crisis management, as well as first aid.

ZSS maintains a high client retention rate that is due to the ability to understand, interpret and exceed client expectations in ensuring the placement of competent and committed officers and supervisors.

Services We Offer

Comprehensive Security Solutions for Every Need
🛡️

Static Guarding

Highly competent dedicated officers for corporations, private sector, residential home functions and events nationwide. Professional attitude and appearance guaranteed.

🖥️

24/7 Control Centre

Real-time monitoring of security officers via Guard monitoring system (Sentinel) with full audit trails. Constant communication between all units.

🚨

Tactical Response

Advanced vehicle tracking and tactical support units responding to critical situations. Professionally trained in tactical training, fire prevention, first aid.

🔍

Investigations & Polygraph

Expert investigators trained in Forensic Psycho-physiology. Pre-employment vetting, theft, fraud, infidelity testing. American Polygraph Association affiliated.

📱

TSU Protect App

Mobile panic button linked to 24/7 Monitoring Centre. Features: Man Down, Journey, Meeting Alarm, Report, Bluetooth Panic Button. ER24 Emergency Medical Response.

👥

Riot & Crowd Control

SASSETA accredited training in crowd dynamics, communication channels, and public order management. 5-7 day certified courses available.

🔔

Alarm Systems

Installation of residential/business alarm systems (Ajax, Hikvision, Paradox) with motion detectors, glass break detectors, and panic buttons.

🧹

Cleaning Services

Professional cleaning for residential, commercial, or industrial sectors with eco-friendly products, 24/7 availability, and SHEQ compliance.
